Po-Hsu Chen, a graphic designer born in 1997 in Taipei, Taiwan.

 

For me, the atmosphere and the viewer's feelings have always been at the core of the design.I try to make my creations full of oriental culture, and so is design. I like to deal with details subtly that makes my visual presentation highly exquisite and full of metaphors. I am used to using new materials and print methods to bring viewers a novel experience.My creative practices are not only stood on the basis of aesthetics, but they also gave viewers different kinds of feeling based on their different contexts.

 

FOREWORD

 

In his works, I always think that his projects are like onions; lots of layers exist in his works. Like holding a secret, you should discern it slowly and carefully; lots of details are beneath his works.
