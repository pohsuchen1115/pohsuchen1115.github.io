Introduction /

The "unmailed letter" focuses on the mixed communication of text and medium. The letter what I wrote was thinking about the relationship between the author(writer and sender) and the audience(reader and recipient).

Design Story /

The letter's content was a little private, so it is slightly difficult to read. In contrast, the leaves are the most distinct. The audiences, readers, or even the recipient who read the letter can feel the color of the leaves fade away over time. I even use the laser print's power to control the words' depth on the acrylics' front and back to represent whether the words are important or not. On the other hand, based on this letter being personal, I also hid some details that only I know.

Design Writing /  Chen Po-Hsu     Year /  2022
