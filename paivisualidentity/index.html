Brief

Plant an Ideo is a brand that combines plants and clothes.

 

Logo

Because of the evolution of media, I prefer to make this logo simply, and it can make various possible changes in digital, printing and every festival.

 

Print

Based on the plant growing is unique. I tried to use "combined printing" to make the difference in the color of front & back. All of the print methods I used were to make consequent of the business cards look more like leafs; the consequently of the print methods I chose were unpredictable, just like every plant growing is not something the plant carer can control.
